Cent text

Cent text lets you set the cent value of a semitone.

To enable Cent text, right-click inside a track and enable "Cent text". 

      
Cent text format: xx, where xx goes between 1 and 99.

  • To remove a cent text (same as setting cent to 0), press DEL.
  • Cents are not supported by:
    • VST instruments
    • AU instruments
    • The FluidSynth instrument
    • The MIDI instrument.
